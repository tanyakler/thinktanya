welcome to my creative playground
11/21/2023 musings

Hi there, I'm Tanya! Welcome to my new website and creative space, thinktanya.com. This is not just a website; it's a canvas where my ideas come to life and find their wings. Here, I let my ideas roam free and document the whimsical places they take me and embrace the magic that unfolds when creativity takes the driver's seat in all my pursuits.

As a recent graduate from Northeastern University, I find myself eagerly navigating the post-grad terrain, excited about the adventures that lie ahead. This website is a manifestation of my passion for design, development, and creation, serving as a platform to share my unique concepts and insights with the world.

The heart of thinktanya.com lies in the Concepts page, where I unleash a spectrum of ideas—from marketing pitches to music video concepts and everything in between. This is the space where my visions become tangible, and I can't wait to take you along for the ride.

The Blog page is my personal corner of expression—a place to share my thoughts, ranging from movie reviews and reflections on fashion news to stories, anecdotes, and love letters to the things that inspire me. It's a glimpse into my stream of consciousness, a space where I share what's occupying the real estate of my mind.

Thank you for embarking on this journey with me and welcome to my creative playground. I hope you find joy, inspiration, and a touch of magic in the ideas and musings I bring to life. Cheers to creativity, expression, and the exciting adventures ahead!

Xo, Tanya
