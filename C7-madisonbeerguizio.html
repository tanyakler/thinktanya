Madison Beer X Guizio
 a collaboration featuring Madison Beer as the new face of Guizio

I love Madison Beer and her music. I love Danielle Guizio and her clothes. Why not put the two together?

Danielle Guizio's brand is a celebration of fearless femininity and raw vulnerability, channeling the energy of the vibrant city of New York. Madison Beer, known for her soul-stirring vocals and a penchant for bold individuality, seamlessly aligns with the spirit of Guizio's designs, having donned them with grace on various occasions.

This collaboration includes a collection on the Guizio website featuring Madison Beer's curated personal favorites from the fashion label. To mark Madison's ascent as the new face of Guizio, a new music video would drop for her single "Home to Another One" featuring the dazzling pieces from the collaboration. The collection, showcased on a dedicated page on the Guizio website and in the new video, becomes a testament to the fusion of the two creative powerhouses.

The music video concept takes center stage—a fashion show where Madison Beer plays the starring role of every model. The narrative unfolds with shots of Madison singing into the mirror during the first verse, gradually transitioning to her strutting down the runway in Guizio's signature pieces as the chorus ignites. The runway spectacle is witnessed by a sea of photographers, fervently capturing Madison's every move, while a disconcerted male lead sits front-row, uncomfortable by the stinging words aimed at him. The crescendo of the show sees Madison bringing out Danielle Guizio after the finale, the culmination of a spectacular collaboration that deserves a bow.

Deliverables include web wireframes of the homepage and collection page featuring the collab, instagram posts(collab teaser, music video announcement, collab announcement), a mock ig feed, and a mock youtube thumbnail for the music video.

This union, a match made in heaven, promises to resonate with fans and enthusiasts alike, creating a spectacle that transcends artistic boundaries. Madison Beer and Danielle Guizio—two trailblazing artists converging in perfect harmony to craft an unforgettable experience that will captivate, inspire, and leave an indelible mark on the realms of fashion and music.
